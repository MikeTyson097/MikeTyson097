# 👨‍💻 My Journey

I grew up in a small village where careers like cybersecurity were unknown to me.  
I spent my early years fixing simple devices and developing a strong problem-solving mindset, without fully understanding the world of technology.

Later, I joined the **military** and served in the defense sector.  
During my service, I observed how **communication systems** operated and how critical secure infrastructure was. This experience sparked my deep interest in cybersecurity.

After completing my service, I began learning **programming**, initially focusing on **Frontend development**.  
However, my long-term goal was always **Cybersecurity**.

Over time, I transitioned fully into the security field — focusing on **web vulnerabilities, penetration testing, and security automation**.

Today, I work as a **Penetration Tester**, identifying vulnerabilities and helping organizations strengthen their security.
